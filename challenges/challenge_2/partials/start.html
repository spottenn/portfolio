Requirements:

  • mouse
  • fixed (non-moving) webcam
  • laptop or desktop

Recommended:

  • good lighting
  • full screen window
  • sense of excitement

To Calibrate:

  1. Sit so that your face fills most or all of the green box in the selfie view, without going over the edge of the edge of the selfie view.
  2. Once you hit the "Start Calibration" button, click on the 4 corners of the page and continue clicking in various places for 30 seconds.
  3. As you are clicking, follow the cursor with your eyes and head+.
  4. Also while you are clicking, move the mouse at only a slow to moderate pace.
